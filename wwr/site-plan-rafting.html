Overview

Rafting the Rapids

Do you like adventure? Do you want an experiance of a lifetime? Do you like trying new things? You have come to the right place. Come with one of our professional trainers and experiance nature to its fullest. Feel the breeze in your hair. Feel the water rushing by. Learn to take on hard things with a group of friends and or family. The raft is a 1-4 days trip depending on what you would like to experiance. Overnight camping and exploring nature. You will pull the raft off to the shore and be able to go hiking and exploring where ever you want. There are pools of water where you can go swimming on these hot days. If you are lucky you can see fishes while riding down the rapids with great speed. Wild life everywhere and amazing night skys filled with starts. Most imporatantly you will have each other on this glorious ride and will need to work as a team. Happy Rafting!

Everyone is Welcome

Anyone and everyone is welcome to come rafting down our river. We have easy river rapids where you and your children of any age can ride with out any worry of falling into the river. It is our child friendly rapids and this is for a day. You go as a family and at the end you end up at a beautiful beach to spend time with one another at. For the older kids(teenagers and older) we have our more basic and advanced rapids where you can spend a day or a few riding. The rapids are more adventures and have the chances of thrill to fill your soal. Then there is our 18+ rapids where they are much stronger than the normal and it requires team work. Bring your sunscreen, towels, food, snacks, ect. This will be a trip you will never forget.

All rapid oppertunities do present the chance to go hiking and explore the unknown if you want. Wild life is everywhere you go. Please keep your children safe and prepared. Expect to see bears in the trees or a snake slithering around. Watch as the fish swim with you as you ride the repids to the beaches.

How much is the trips? The adventure for the young is $20 a person. The Normal rapids is $25 a person. The experianced is $30 a person.

What is the bare minimum and maximum of the trips? The shortest is a day trip. The longest is 4 Days. We make sure everyone is ready and prepared for which ever trip they desire to do.

What kind of wild life will we experiance? You could see bears, snakes, mountain lions, gophers, all kinds of birds, ect. Bring your camera and take lots of pictures.

All your answers can be found here on the website. If you dont see it our Rapid Team Leaders will be able to help you.
